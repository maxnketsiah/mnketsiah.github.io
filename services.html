Services

  • Bookkeeping

    Bookkeeping is a must for every business. It helps measure the company’s financial progress as well providing useful accounting information for decision making. It is a requirement by Tax Authorities for every business to keep proper books of accounts.

  • Accounting Software

    We have developed a system for keeping proper books of accounts. It was designed specifically for small businesses who cannot afford fully fledged accounting software which comes at a high cost. The features of the system are daybooks, general journal, ledgers, income statement, statement of financial position and statement of changes in equity. It also has features for keeping track of your tax liabilities.

  • Payroll

    We have a system for managing your payroll. The system calculates employees’ net salaries, P.A.Y.E, and pension contribution

  • Tax Filing

    We assist companies and businesses in filing their tax return on time to avoid heavy sanctions by the Ghana Revenue Authority. We assist in filing: VAT Returns GETFUND/NHIL Returns Withholding Tax Returns Pay As You Earn Annual Income Tax Return Self Assessment Tax Planning

  • Business Plan

    Business plan is essential when starting a business. It contains all the information and plan for turning a business idea into real business. We assist individuals and businesses to put together a business plan.

  • Company Secretarial Services

    The company secretary works closely with the Board of directors. The primary responsibility of the company secretary is recording of minutes, issuing notices and filing statutory documents. The company secretary also advise shareholders and directors on any changes and development in the laws affecting the operations of the company. The company secretary also has the responsibility of maintaining the statutory register of the company. We offer the following company secretarial services: Appointment of directors, keeping database of directors, registration of new companies, drafting of board resolutions, drafting of shareholders’ resolution, organizing annual general meeting, corporate governance advisory, recording of board minutes, scheduling and coordinating of board meetings
